- Attal is a project of turn-based strategy game.
Rules will be inspired of a famous game created by 3DO : "Heroes of Might and Magic III". You can play in stand-alone mode (against AI) or against other human player through a network.
This game will be open and themeable, in order to allow new graphics, new rules, new campaigns and new scenarii. We will even provide a map editor and a scenario's editor.

Current graphics (created by our team) are included in the "Cyberpunk" theme. We plan to develop (later) another theme "medieval" (with adapted rules).

- We want to achieve a complete, open and high quality game :

Complete -> We don't want to just develop a game engine but a real game, fully playable, with tested rules. It will be possible to play in solo or multiplayer mode, with campaigns, editor...
Open -> Developped in "Open Source", le goal of Attal is not to earn money (sniff...) but to show that it is possible to make good game, free and open.
High Quality -> We want to realize a game that could be compared with commercial one. (but, of course, we will try to do it better)

This (great) projetct is quite new (august 2000) but we have clear ideas on what to do and how. (look at our Roadmap).

The team is currently composed of 5 (motivated) people... but we will welcome other members.
