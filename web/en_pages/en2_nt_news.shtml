Welcome.
Attal: Lords of Doom is a video game still in development.
It's a turn-based strategy game, under Linux (for the moment) that can be played alone (against AI) or against other through a network (local or internet).

If you want to join this project, don't hesitate to contact us.

You can also look at the project's page on Sourceforge.net (where you can also suscribe to our mailing list, submit bugs or browse our CVS.
